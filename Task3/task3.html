NB:You must specify at least one of top, right, bottom or left for all positioning to work.

Sticky position

The element behaves like relative until it reaches a specified scroll position, then it sticks

Fixed position

The element is positioned relative to the viewport and stays fixed even when scrolling.

Absolute position

The element is positioned relative to its nearest positioned ancestor (relative, absolute, or fixed)

Relative position

The element is positioned relative to its original place in the document.
